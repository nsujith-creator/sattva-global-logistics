**Finding 1: `/trade-advisory` title too long — causes SERP truncation**

Evidence: Current title in `dist/trade-advisory/index.html`: "Freight Market Intelligence — India Gulf & Red Sea Export Advisory | Sattva Global Logistics" = 94 characters. Google's display limit is ~60–65 chars. Visible in SERP: "Freight Market Intelligence — India Gulf & Red Sea Exp..." — drops Sattva and the meaningful trailing context.

Diagnosis: Searchers clicking from Gulf shipping, surcharge, or carrier update queries see a non-descriptive truncated snippet. This is the most plausible mechanical driver of the known high-impression / low-CTR pattern on this page. Meta description (180 chars) is well-written and does not truncate badly.

Recommended action — shorten title to under 65 chars, leading with trade lane context. Three options:

- Option A: "Gulf & Red Sea Freight Update — India Export Advisory | Sattva" (62 chars)
- Option B: "India Gulf Shipping Intelligence — FCL Export Advisory | Sattva" (63 chars)
- Option C: "Gulf, Red Sea & Africa Freight Update | Sattva Global Logistics" (63 chars)
